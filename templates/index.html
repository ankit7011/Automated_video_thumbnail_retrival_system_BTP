ABOUT
Given a long format video, our goal is to rank the frames of the video in terms of their suitability of being a video thumbnail candidate. We will score each frame of the video in terms of its suitability to being a thumbnail candidate, and select the best frame as the thumbnail of the video.

Extracting Frames
We have extracted frames from the video (720 x 1080).The video taken for demonstration is 30fps and we have collected every fourth frame from the video.

Clustering Faces
All the faces collected using the MTCNN model are clustered using the Agglomerative Clustering algorithm, with centroid linkage for calculating distance between clusters.

Character Interaction Graph
This helps in registering the interaction between characters that will account for a score in deciding whether to choose them for the thumbnail.

Scoring Frames
We have calculated 3 scores, prominence score, expression score and landscape score, which helps in getting the best thumbnails for the video.
